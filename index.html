Snakes and Ladders

Game Rules

This game contains a board that is numbered from 1 to 100 and the player's first one to reach number 100 will be considered as winner. Each player will have its turn to roll a dice that contains values numbered 1 to 6 and the based on that dice value, the player will make a movement from its position to a new position. There is also a bonus for the player who's dice stop at number 6. He will make 6 forward movements and then he will get another chance to roll the dice to move to newer position. The game will be easier for the player who stop at a position that is bottom of a ladder because the ladder will take him up and he will be skipping some rows of the board making him more closer to position 100. However, If player is unlucky at and stopped at a position that is a head of the snake, then he will be taken down to the position that is the tail of the snake and he will be losing his position that he struggled to reach it and he might being taken down falling from many high rows.
